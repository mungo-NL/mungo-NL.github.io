Explore the geology of Newfoundland in Minecraft! 

We are in the final stretches of building a Minecraft world with geography and geology based on the island of Newfoundland. This world was built using publicly available elevation and geology maps. The geologic map used in the creation of this world is the 1:1 million bedrock geology map of Newfoundland, found on [GeoAtlas] (https://geoatlas.gov.nl.ca/Default.htm).
