## Welcome to MUNGO

MUNGO is the Memorial University of Newfoundland and Labrador's Geophysics Outreach. We are geophysicists, geoscientists and educators in Newfoundland and Labrador who are excited to share geophysics with our communities.

Our mission is to bring awareness and understanding of geophysics through engaging outreach programs, educational resources, and community partnerships.
