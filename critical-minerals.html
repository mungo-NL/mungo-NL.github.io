Critical Minerals in Newfoundland and Labrador

Newfoundland and Labrador is known for its rich deposits of critical minerals that are essential for various modern technologies, including electric vehicles, renewable energy systems, and advanced electronics.

These minerals include lithium, cobalt, nickel, and rare earth elements, which are increasingly important for achieving a sustainable future.

The province's geological formations present significant opportunities for exploration and development of these resources, fostering economic growth and contributing to global efforts in mineral sustainability.
